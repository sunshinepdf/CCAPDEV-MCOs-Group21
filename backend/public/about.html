About Animo Commons

Purpose

Animo Commons is designed to give everyone a place to post, discover, and interact with content. Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive space for daily discussions and sharing knowledge.

Features

  • Post updates, questions, or articles.
  • Discover popular and trending posts.
  • Engage with comments and upvotes/downvotes.
  • Personalized profiles and user dashboards.

Technologies & Libraries

Backend (Node.js):

  • bcryptjs
  • connect-mongo
  • cors
  • cropperjs
  • dotenv
  • express
  • express-handlebars
  • express-session
  • hbs

Third-Party Libraries:
